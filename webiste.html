The Great Barrier Reef

The Great barrier reef is the world's largest coral reef system, located off the coast of Queensland, Australia.

Here are some fascinating facts about the Great Barrier Reef:

  1. It is compased of over 2,900 individual reefs and 900 islands.
  2. It is home to a diverse range of marine life,including over 1,500 species of fish.
  3. It can be seen from outer space
