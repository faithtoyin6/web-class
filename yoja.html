THE HEALTH BENEFITS OF YOGA

Yoga, an ancient practice that originated in india, has been passed down through generations for thousands of years.In recent decades, Yoga has gained popularity worldwide for its numerous health benefits,both physical and mental.

Physical Benefits

  • Improved flexiblity and range of motion
  • Increased strength and muscle tone
  • Better posture and alignment
  • Enchanced balance and coordination
  • Alleviation of chronic pain

Mental Benefits

  • Reduced stress and anxiety
  • Improved mood and emotional well-being
  • Enchanced focus and concentration
  • Greater self-awareness and mindfulness
